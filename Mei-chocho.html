Mei chocho
Artocarpus mariannensis Trecul   

Mei chocho whole fruit

Photo © Jim Wiseman
 
 
 
Geographic Origin of Accession: Federated States of Micronesia
Locality: Chuuk
Other Names: Dugdug, Chebei, Mei kole, Te mai
Notes: Mei chocho is a type of seeded breadfruit or "Meias." It is a very common atoll variety, along with Mei koeng. It grows to a very large stature and trunk size, and is used to make canoes. It doesn't reach as massive a size when grown in the Lagoon islands.
Season: February - March, May - October
