Geographic Origin of Accession: Tokelau
Locality: Nukunonu
Notes: Grown from seed. The parent tree is a natural hybrid between Ulu hamoa (Artocarpus altilis) a seeded variety from Samoa, and Ulu elise (Artocarpus mariennensis) originally from the Tuvalu (Ellice Islands) and considered to most resemble its Ulu hamoa parentage.
