Artocarpus altilis (Parkinson) Fosberg

Geographic Origin of Accession: Federated States of Micronesia
Locality: Pohnpei
Other Names: Ikunlal, Porohiti
Distribution: Kosrae, Society Islands
Notes: Popular variety, usually grown close to the house because it is a small tree and the fruit are easy to reach. Usually prepared by baking or roasting. When steamed or boiled the light yellow flesh is tender and soft and easy to mash.
