Ulu fiti
Artocarpus altilis (Parkinson) Fosberg   

Ulu fiti whole fruit

Photo © Jim Wiseman
 
 
Geographic Origin of Accession: Samoa
Locality: former SPC Regional Breadfruit Collection, Samoa: Upolu
Other Names: Ulu falaoa
Distribution: Variety from Rotuma, introduced to Samoa in early 1960s.
Notes: The yellow flesh is tender and slightly sweet when mature. Has a tender, moist texture when cooked. Excellent for chips or "french fries."
Season: May - October
